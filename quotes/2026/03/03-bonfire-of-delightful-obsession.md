---
title: "Bonfire of Delightful Obsession"
date: "2026-03-03"
---

> They tell you to find your passion, to gently nurture its flame. Nonsense! True enthusiasm isn't a delicate ember; it's a glorious, slightly chaotic bonfire, often fueled by an obsession others deem delightfully unhinged. Forget the quiet contemplation; unleash the inner pyromaniac, the one who sees a challenge and thinks, 'Oh, this will be fun!' Don't just follow your passion; kidnap it, paint it neon, and take it for a joyride. The world doesn't need another polite spark; it craves the hilarious, infectious blaze of someone genuinely thrilled to be making a glorious racket.
