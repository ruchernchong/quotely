date: "2026-05-20"

> We often mistake purpose for a treasure hidden in the world, waiting to be unearthed by diligent search. But meaning isn't discovered; it is an audacious act of will, a silent rebellion against the void. It’s not about finding your path, but about forging it with resolute intention, declaring your significance in a universe that offers none by default.
