title: "Lostness Reveals the Way"
date: "2026-05-09"
---

> True exploration begins not with a map, but with the courage to be utterly lost. For in the vast wilderness of the unknown, where comfort dissolves, one finds the most profound compass within. The deepest journeys are not to distant lands, but into the expansive silence of our own becoming, where the world is both discovered and gently forgotten, only to be remembered anew.
