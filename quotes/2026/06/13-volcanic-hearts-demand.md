date: "2026-06-13"

> True passion isn't a gentle breeze nudging you forward, nor a quiet whisper in the dark. It's the relentless, untamed river within, a torrent that carves new canyons and reshapes landscapes. It's the volcanic core erupting, not in a polite, controlled burst, but with a primal, irresistible force that demands an outlet. Embrace that burning current, that molten enthusiasm, for it's the only compass that never lies, the only fuel that burns brighter with every obstacle, and the only path that truly leads you to where you are meant to be, scars and triumphs intertwined.
