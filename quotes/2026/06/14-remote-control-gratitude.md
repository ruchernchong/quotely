title: "Remote Control Gratitude"
date: "2026-06-14"
---

> One might observe that our internal complaint department is always open, yet the gratitude hotline often goes straight to voicemail. We're quick to notice the pebble in our shoe, but often overlook the marvel of having two feet to walk with. Perhaps if we celebrated our everyday wonders with the same enthusiasm we reserve for discovering a misplaced remote, our appreciation batteries would never run dry.
