> A river, born of a mountain's dream, does not question the stone. It simply flows, an unyielding current, shaping canyons with persistent grace. Each obstacle becomes a sculptor's tool, not a barrier. So let your ambition, like water, sculpt its own grand design, transforming resistance into the very bedrock of your unique ascent.
