> True freedom is not merely the absence of external chains, but the liberation from self-imposed limitations and the echoes of unexamined beliefs. It is observing the subtle patterns of conformity and choosing a path dictated by one's own reasoned spirit, understanding that the most profound independence blossoms from within, a continuous act of conscious self-governance.
