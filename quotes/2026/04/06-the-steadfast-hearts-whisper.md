> Remember, darling heart, that your deepest roots are forged in the quiet moments of enduring. Every tremor you've weathered has not diminished your light, but rather polished its resilience. Trust the gentle strength within you; it whispers tales of unwavering resolve, guiding you to bloom beautifully, even after the longest night.
