---
title: "Embrace the Leash of Liberty"
date: "2025-12-23"
---

> True freedom isn't escaping all masters, but gleefully becoming the funniest, most demanding master of yourself. Independence isn't doing whatever you please, but playfully picking your own glorious chains and then celebrating their perfect fit. You're free only when you realize your greatest strength is your chosen weakness, and your independence is utterly dependent on your own peculiar eccentricities.
