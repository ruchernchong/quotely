---
title: "The Unbound Larva's Vow"
date: "2025-12-29"
---

> The old world whispered, become a butterfly. But the larva, feeling an ancient hum, decided its wings would be carved from the forgotten skies, its colors drawn from defiant sunsets. It didn't spin the cocoon of conformity; it wove a new tapestry of becoming. Let others follow the map; this creature charted its own cosmic dance, transforming not just form, but the very definition of flight.
