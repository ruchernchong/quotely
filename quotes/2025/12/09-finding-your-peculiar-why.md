> Humans are curious creatures, always rummaging through life's cosmic junk drawer, hoping to find the instruction manual for their own existence. The truth is, your purpose often isn't a bold proclamation, but a quirky little hum only you can hear. It's less about a grand divine plan and more about finding the perfect puzzle piece that fits your oddly shaped soul, even if that piece is just making exceptional toast.
