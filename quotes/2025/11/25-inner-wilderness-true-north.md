> Forget the clamor of the crowd; true insight rarely whispers amidst applause. Solitude isn't an absence of company; it's the raw, unvarnished presence of self. Here, the comfortable illusions crumble, leaving stark truth. Don't seek comfort; seek the unshakeable foundation built in the quiet, brutal honesty of your own mind. This is where your actual strength is forged.
