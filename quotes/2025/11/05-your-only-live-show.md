---
title: "Your Only Live Show"
date: "2025-11-05"
---

> Our minds, diligent comedians, constantly replay yesterday's bloopers or frantically script tomorrow's worries. This spectacle blinds us to the vibrant, hilarious show happening now. We're time-travel experts, missing the live plot. Dismiss the inner director, drop the script, and simply enjoy this spontaneous, unrepeatable moment. It's the only live show, and you're front row.
